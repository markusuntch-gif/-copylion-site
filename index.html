Problem

Good AI text gets scattered fast.

The best parts of AI conversations often arrive as fragments: a sentence, prompt, outline, title, answer, or useful phrasing. CopyLion gives those pieces a focused home outside endless chat histories, notes, and clipboard noise.

Who it is for

Built for people who reuse ideas.

  • AI power users saving strong answers and workflows
  • Copywriters and creators collecting reusable lines
  • Prompt collectors building a practical prompt library
  • Researchers keeping useful fragments from many tools
  • Anyone who wants a lighter place than a full notes app

Privacy-first

Your fragments stay on your device.

CopyLion has no account, no login, no analytics, no tracking, no ads, and no server-side fragment storage. Share Sheet, Shortcuts, and clipboard actions are used only when you choose to pass text into the app.
