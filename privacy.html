Privacy Summary

Your fragments are stored locally on your device. CopyLion only imports text when you explicitly choose to add it. CopyLion does not monitor your clipboard in the background, does not require an account, does not use analytics or tracking, and does not share your text fragments with third parties.

Data We Collect

CopyLion stores the text fragments and archived collections you create or explicitly import into the app. This content is saved locally on your device so you can collect fragments, reorder them, archive collections, restore archived collections, and copy a final text.

Data We Do Not Collect

CopyLion does not collect account information, contact lists, location data, advertising identifiers, browsing history, or usage analytics. CopyLion does not sell your data.

Clipboard, Share Sheet, and Shortcuts

CopyLion does not monitor your clipboard in the background. CopyLion only imports text when you explicitly choose to add it, such as tapping an import or paste action inside the app, sharing selected content through the iOS Share Sheet, or running a Shortcut that passes text to CopyLion.

The Share Sheet only imports the content you choose to share. Shortcuts only import the text you explicitly pass to the Shortcut.

Local Storage

Your fragments are stored locally on your device. CopyLion does not upload your fragments to a CopyLion server and does not provide account-based online storage.

Third-Party Sharing

CopyLion does not share your text fragments with third parties. CopyLion does not sell your data.

Analytics and Tracking

CopyLion does not use analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising SDKs, cookies, or behavior profiling.

Children and Minors

CopyLion is a general utility app and is not directed to children. CopyLion does not knowingly collect personal information from children or minors.

Data Deletion

You can delete fragments and archived collections inside the app. You can also remove CopyLion from your device to delete locally stored app data, subject to normal iOS backup and device behavior.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if CopyLion changes in a way that affects privacy. The effective date will be updated when the policy changes.
