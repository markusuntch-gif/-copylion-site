FAQ

Does CopyLion upload my text?

No. Your fragments are stored locally on your device. CopyLion does not upload your text to a CopyLion server.

Does CopyLion monitor my clipboard?

No. CopyLion does not monitor your clipboard in the background. It only imports text when you explicitly choose to add it, use the iOS Share Sheet, or run a Shortcut that passes text to CopyLion.

How do I delete fragments?

Open a collection, select the fragment you want to remove, and use the delete action. Deleted fragments are removed from the local collection.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Settings in CopyLion and tap Restore Purchase. Restore works for the Apple Account that originally purchased CopyLion Unlimited.
